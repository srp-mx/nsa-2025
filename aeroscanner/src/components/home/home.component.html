About us

AeroScan is a digital application designed to help Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in the heavy industry sector overcome a key barrier to securing contracts.

It allows SMEs to generate estimated emission reports based on their location, providing a low-cost, initial validation of their environmental impact.

Accelerated Compliance

Facilitates the application process for environmental certifications such as ISO 14001, through auditable evidence and proactive NOM alerts.

Time Related Statistics

Obtain and store historical reports based on periods of interest, or check the current air quality status.
